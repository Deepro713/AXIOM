## Tools
- 3D printer (PETG and ABS capable, with fine nozzle for 0.1mm layer height)
- Soldering iron with fine tip
- Solder (fine gauge)
- Flux
- Desoldering wick/pump
- Multimeter (for continuity and voltage checks)
- Precision screwdriver set (M2, M3 sizes)
- Wire cutters/strippers
- Tweezers (for small components and heat-set inserts)
- Heat gun (optional, for heat-set inserts)

## Assumptions
- Builder has basic soldering skills for surface-mount components and pin headers
- Access to a computer for flashing firmware and testing software
- Familiarity with basic electronics and mechanical assembly principles
- A suitable USB-C power adapter for initial power-up and charging
